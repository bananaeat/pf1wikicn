# **高等魔力通道** *Eldritch conduit, greater*

**学派** 变化系 

**环位** 术士/法师 6, 女巫 6

## 🪄 施放

**施法时间** 1 标准动作

**成分** 语言, 姿势, 材料

## ✨ 效果  

**范围** one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart

**距离** 中距 (100尺+10尺/等级)  

**持续时间** 1 minute/level 

**豁免** Will negates

**法术抗力** 是

## 📖 描述

This spell functions like @Compendium[pf1.spells.fwbgmfya11gtko14]{eldritch conduit} save for the differences listed above and that the target must be within medium range (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) in order for you to use the conduit. Using a creature as a conduit for a spell ends the greater eldritch conduit effect on that creature, but does not end the effect for other eldritch conduits.
