## 🪄 施放

**施法时间** 1 标准动作

**成分** 语言, 姿势, 材料

## ✨ 效果 

**目标** one spell effect 

**距离** 接触  

**持续时间** instantaneous plus 1 minute/level (see text) 

**豁免** Will negates (harmless)

This spell functions like absorb rune I, but it can affect runes equivalent to a spell of 5th level or lower.
