# **Heroic fortune, mass** *Heroic fortune, mass*

**学派** 塑能系 

**环位** 牧师/先知 5, 吟游诗人 4

## 🪄 施放

**施法时间** 1 标准动作

**成分** 语言, 姿势, 材料, 圣徽

## ✨ 效果 

**目标** one or more creatures, no two of which can be more  than 30 ft. apart 

**距离** 近距 (25尺+5尺/2等级)  

**持续时间** 1 round/level 

**豁免** Will negates (harmless)

**法术抗力** 否

## 📖 描述

This spell functions like heroic fortune, except as noted above.
