    # **思维护盾ii** *Thought shield ii*

    **学派** 防护系 

    **环位** 异能者 3

    ## 🪄 施放

    **施法时间** 1 immediate

    **成分** 语言

    ## ✨ 效果 

    **目标** you 

    **距离** 自身  

    **持续时间** 1 round 

    **法术抗力** 否

    ## 📖 描述

    This functions as thought shield I, but the circumstance bonus on Will saves to resist mind-affecting effects increases to +6. This spell can be undercast.
